    Ganesha Chaturthi is the 10 day-long Hindu festival held on the birthday of the LORD GANESH... He is the son of Shiva and Parvati.It is celebrated all over India.The festival is held on shukla chaturthi in the Hindu month of Bhaadrapada and ends on Anant chaturdashi.For example in 2020 it will be celebrated on August 22th
    Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated across India, especially in states like Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Goa and Karnataka...

    festival of Ganesh Chaturthi finds its origin in the Maratha reign, with Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj starting the festival in Mumbai, Maharashtra...

    It is celebrated widely in Maharashtra. On this occasion people make special "modaks" which are loved by Lord Ganesha. Lalbaug is a place that every year celebrates Ganesh Chaturthi on a large scale... There are Ganesha's form like Ganesh, Lalbaugchya Raja, Siddhivinayak Maharaj, and Dhagru Sheth
    The most popular ones are: Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Mandir and Lalbaugcha Raja...
    India's biggest Ganesh is at Khairatabad locality of Hyderabad, India...

    Celebrations include four stages of rituals:
    Pranapratistha --> It involves the positioning of the idol or deity of Lord Ganesha into the pandals.They play with colours as well...
    Shodasopachara --> It involves praying to the lord and offering him pooja...
    Uttarpuja --> After this ritual, the idol is moved among the public so that they can take his blessings...
    Ganpati Visarjan --> In this the idol is immersed into the river, sea or ocean.He is believed to go back to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati after the immersion...
